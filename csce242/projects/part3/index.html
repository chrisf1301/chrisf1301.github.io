Welcome to Oktoberfest

Every year, the Vereinigung Erzgebirge Club in Warminster hosts the beloved Bucks-Mont Oktoberfest, renowned as Bucks & Montgomery Counties' longest running German festival. The 2025 edition—its 88th—takes place on Saturday, September 6, 2025, running from 11:00 AM to 10:00 PM at 130 Davisville Road.
