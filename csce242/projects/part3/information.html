Event Information

Join us for the 88th annual Bucks-Mont Oktoberfest, the longest running German festival in Bucks & Montgomery Counties. This beloved tradition brings together families and friends for a day of authentic German culture, food, and entertainment.

Experience traditional German music, dance performances, and cultural demonstrations throughout the day. Our vendors offer a wide variety of authentic German foods including bratwurst, sauerkraut, pretzels, and of course, traditional German beer.

Don't miss the children's activities, craft vendors, and the famous stein-holding contest. This family-friendly event has something for everyone, from toddlers to seniors, making it the perfect way to celebrate German heritage and community spirit.

Admission is free, with food and beverages available for purchase. We encourage you to bring your own lawn chairs and blankets for seating. Parking is available on-site, and the event is held rain or shine under our covered pavilion.
